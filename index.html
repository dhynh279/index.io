Why Weight Training?

Strength training builds muscle, increases metabolism, improves bone density, and enhances overall physical performance.

Getting Started with Lifting

Follow these steps to begin your weight training journey safely and effectively:

  1. Assess your current fitness level
  2. Learn proper form for basic exercises
  3. Start with lighter weights to master technique
  4. Create a balanced workout schedule
  5. Track your progress consistently

Essential Weight Training Equipment

  • Barbells and weight plates
  • Dumbbells
  • Power rack or squat stand
  • Bench press station
  • Pull-up bar
