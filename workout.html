How to Use the Tracker

  1. Click "Start Workout" to begin the timer and animation
  2. Click "Add Weight" to simulate adding plates to the barbell
  3. Watch the animation to see proper lifting form
  4. Track your progress with the live statistics
  5. Use "Pause" to take breaks and "Reset" to start over

Workout Tips

  • Always warm up before heavy lifting
  • Maintain proper form to prevent injury
  • Increase weight gradually (5-10 lbs per week)
  • Rest 60-90 seconds between sets
  • Stay hydrated throughout your workout
