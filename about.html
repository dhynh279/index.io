About Mustangs Fitness

We are dedicated to helping students build strength, confidence, and healthier lifestyles through proper weight training.

Our Mission

To educate and empower people of all fitness levels to safely and effectively incorporate weight training into their lives, debunking myths and promoting evidence-based practices.

Our Story

Our program, Mustangs Fitness, began as a small program to introduce students who want to improve their figure/lifestyle but have never worked out before. Our goal is to teach students fundamentals to weight training and maintaining their figure all while not needing to set aside 2+ hours of their precious study time.

What We Offer

  • Beginner to advanced training programs
  • Form and technique tutorials
  • Nutrition guidance for strength athletes
  • Personal training certifications
  • Community strength competitions

Our Team

Our certified instructors include former competitive powerlifters, bodybuilders, and strength coaches with decades of combined experience in the fitness industry.

Training Philosophy

We believe in progressive overload, proper form above all else, and the importance of recovery in any effective training program.
